What is Emotional Intelligence?

For me Emotional Intelligence is self-awareness which is your Identity, to know and understanding who you are and where you come from, strenghtens you from the inside out.

This guy puts it down like this:

The 5 Components/Elements/Domains of the EQ Model, According to Daniel Goleman, there are five components or elements of emotional intelligence:

  • Self-Awareness
  • Self-Regulation
  • Motivation
  • Empathy
  • Social Skills

How is it different to IQ?

The difference between EQ and IQ. IQ is Intelligence like academic, like brainy people where they can be tested and graded.

EQ is a quality of Self awareness like empathy and intuition, qualities that cannot be measured or tested.

Why is it Important?

Both are important, depending on who you and where you come from.

For me EQ is very much a well being of self, well being of environment, and well being of wairua. Self awareness or Identity strenghtens who I am, respecting who I am and others around me. EQ enables freedom of responsibility and motivation. EQ is also learnt through emotions, the senses, visual, touch etc.

IQ has a different set of talents, which are important to many, which can be measured to see who is clever-er then the others, can even be displayed with a Masters or PHD certificate which can be trained and studied for.
